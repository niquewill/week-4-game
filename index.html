The Crystal Collector


Read the directions below to play the game.




  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • There are four crystals each having a random value. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  • Eachtime when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal
